## 1. Platform Overview

**IT Park Bulletin** is an internal strategic intelligence and collaboration platform built for **IT Park Uzbekistan**. It serves as a centralized hub where department heads and team members can:

- Monitor global and local tech/business news relevant to their department
- Save, organize, and share important articles
- Collaborate via personal messaging
- Track department-specific KPIs, deals, projects, and leads
- Access multi-country legislation databases
- Generate professional PDF bulletin reports
- Manage tasks and team activities

The platform is designed for daily use by all IT Park departments — from export development and investment attraction to marketing, HR, and AI infrastructure.

---

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 Registration

1. Open the platform URL in your browser
2. On the login screen, click **"Register"** tab at the top
3. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Full Name** — your display name
   - **Email** — your work email address
   - **Department** — select your department from the dropdown (28 departments available)
   - **Password** — create a secure password
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. You will be automatically logged in

### 2.2 Login

**Option A — Email/Password:**
1. Enter your registered email and password
2. Click **"Sign In"**

**Option B — Google Sign-In:**
1. Click **"Sign in with Google"**
2. Select your Google account
3. On first login, your profile will be created automatically

### 2.3 First Steps After Login

After logging in, you will see:
- **Sidebar** on the left with navigation tabs
- **Header** at the top with your name, avatar, and action buttons (notification bell, chat, settings, logout)
- **News Feed** as the default view

## 3. User Roles & Permissions

The platform has three user roles:

| Feature | Viewer | Head | Admin |
|---------|--------|------|-------|
| View news feed | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Save/unsave articles | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Share to Telegram | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Generate PDF reports | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Use personal chat | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| View workspace widgets | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Track items (companies, deals, etc.) | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| View own tasks | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Update task status | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| View team members | No | Yes | Yes |
| Assign tasks to team | No | Yes | Yes |
| Create HR announcements | No | No* | Yes |
| Access Admin Panel | No | No | Yes |
| Manage users (roles, departments) | No | No | Yes |
| View platform activity/KPI | No | No | Yes |
| Delete users/content | No | No | Yes |
| Export activity reports | No | No | Yes |

*HR department members can also create announcements regardless of role.

**Default role:** All new users start as **Viewer**. An Admin can promote users to Head or Admin via the Admin Panel.

## 4. News Feed

The **News Feed** is the main landing page. It aggregates tech and business news from global sources via Google News RSS feeds.

### 4.1 Browsing News

Each news card shows:
- Article thumbnail image
- Topic badge (e.g., AI, Startup, Tech, Investment, General)
- Source name and publication date
- Article title and description
- Action buttons: Save, Share to Telegram

### 4.2 Filtering News

Use the filter bar at the top to narrow results:

- **By Topic:** All Topics, AI, Startup, Tech, Investment, General
- **By Department:** Filter news relevant to a specific department
- **By Country:** Click the globe icon to select a country — USA, UK, India, Germany, South Korea, Japan, Russia, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan
- **By Keyword:** Type search terms in the search box and click "Search"

### 4.3 Loading More Articles

Scroll to the bottom and click **"Load More"** to fetch the next batch of 20 articles.

---

## 5. Saving Articles

### 5.1 Save an Article

Click the **"Save"** button (bookmark icon) on any news card. The button will turn active to indicate the article is saved.

### 5.2 View Saved Items

Click **"Saved Items"** in the sidebar to see all your bookmarked articles.

### 5.3 Unsave an Article

In the Saved Items view, click the **"Unsave"** button on any saved article to remove it from your collection.

---

## 6. Share to Telegram

Each news card has a **Telegram share button** (paper plane icon). Clicking it sends the article to the configured IT Park Telegram group chat with:

- Article title (bold)
- Short description (150 characters)
- Topic hashtag
- Source name
- Direct link to the full article

## 7. PDF Report Generation

You can create professional PDF bulletin reports from selected articles.

### How to Generate a Report:

1. **Select articles** — use the checkbox in the top-left corner of each news card to select articles you want to include
2. Once you have selected articles, a **"Generate PDF"** button appears
3. Click it to generate the report

### Report Contents:
- **Cover page** with IT Park branding, date range, and department info
- **Table of contents** with all included articles
- **Article pages** — each article displayed with its title, source, description, and image
- **Page numbers** throughout the document

The PDF will download automatically to your device.

## 8. My Workspace

Click **"My Workspace"** in the sidebar to access department-specific tools. The workspace is customized based on your department — each department sees widgets relevant to their work.

### 8.1 Tracked Items

Track companies, deals, startups, projects, events, and more:

- **Create Item:** Click "Add Item", select the type (company, deal, startup, project, event, registration, lead, market, metric, issue), enter title and description
- **Track Status:** Items can be Active, Completed, or Archived
- **Add Notes:** Click on an item to add timestamped notes for progress tracking
- **Sort & Filter:** Filter by item type and status, sort by creation or update date

**Available Item Types:**
| Type | Use Case |
|------|----------|
| Company | Track target companies |
| Deal | Sales/partnership opportunities |
| Startup | Monitor startups in your domain |
| Project | Ongoing project tracking |
| Event | Upcoming events and conferences |
| Registration | Registration workflows |
| Lead | Sales leads |
| Market | Market research entries |
| Metric | KPI and data points |
| Issue | Problems/blockers to resolve |

### 8.2 AI Daily Brief

An AI-powered personalized daily briefing that includes:
- Summary of your recent work activities
- Relevant industry news for your department
- 3 actionable recommendations
- Today's priority action item

The brief updates once per day and is cached for 3 hours.

### 8.3 Department News Feed

A dedicated news widget showing the latest articles specifically relevant to your department's focus area.

### 8.4 Call Log (Softlanding Department)

For the Softlanding department, a call tracking tool:

- **Log calls:** Enter lead name, company, phone, email
- **Record outcomes:** Answered, No Answer, Callback Requested, Converted, Rejected
- **Interest level:** High, Medium, Low
- **Follow-up scheduling:** Set a follow-up date
- **Office preference:** Select which IT Park office the lead prefers (HQ, Samarkand, CSpace, Shakespeare, Ground Zero)
- **Statistics dashboard:** Today's calls, total calls, pending follow-ups, conversion count

### 8.5 Spravochnik (Knowledge Base)

A department-specific FAQ/knowledge base:

- **Browse entries** by category: Call Scripts, FAQ, Procedures, Other
- **Search** for specific information
- **Create entries** with title, category, and full content
- **Edit/delete** existing entries

### 8.6 Office Directory

View the directory of IT Park offices and co-working spaces with addresses, phone numbers, and websites.

### 8.7 Metrics Dashboard

A quick overview of your workspace activity:
- Number of active tracked items
- Completed items count
- Total notes written

### 8.8 My Team (Head Role Only)

If you are a department Head, you can see all team members in your department with their roles and join dates.

## 9. Task Management System

The task system allows department Heads to assign work to team members, and all users to track their tasks.

### 9.1 For Department Heads

**Assigning Tasks:**
1. Go to **My Workspace**
2. Find the **"Tasks I Assigned"** widget
3. Click **"New Task"**
4. Fill in:
   - **Title** — task name
   - **Description** — detailed instructions
   - **Assign To** — select a team member from your department
   - **Priority** — Low, Medium, High, or Urgent
   - **Deadline** — optional due date
5. Click **"Create Task"**

**Managing Tasks:**
- View all tasks you assigned
- See their current status (Pending, In Progress, Completed)
- Delete tasks if needed

### 9.2 For All Users (Viewers)

**Viewing Your Tasks:**
1. Go to **My Workspace**
2. Find the **"My Tasks"** widget
3. See all tasks assigned to you

**Updating Task Status:**
- Click status buttons to move tasks through the workflow:
  - **Pending** → **In Progress** → **Completed**
- Tasks show priority level with color coding:
  - Red = Urgent
  - Orange = High
  - Yellow = Medium
  - Green = Low
- Overdue tasks are marked with an "overdue" badge

## 10. Personal Chat

The platform includes a personal 1-on-1 messaging system.

### 10.1 Opening Chat

Click the **chat icon** (speech bubble) in the header to open the chat panel. A **red badge** appears on the icon when you have unread messages.

### 10.2 Contact List

The chat panel shows all platform users organized in two sections:
- **Recent** — users you have chatted with, sorted by last message (newest first)
- **By Department** — remaining users grouped by their department

Each contact shows:
- Profile photo and name
- Department label
- Last message preview and timestamp
- Unread message count (if any)

### 10.3 Sending Messages

1. Click on a contact to open the conversation
2. Type your message in the text area at the bottom
3. Press **Enter** to send (press **Shift+Enter** for a new line)
4. Or click the **send button** (arrow icon)

Messages have a maximum length of 2,000 characters.

### 10.4 Message Display

- **Your messages** appear on the right side in blue bubbles
- **Other person's messages** appear on the left in gray bubbles with their avatar
- Messages show the sender's name, department tag, and timestamp
- **Date separators** appear between messages from different days

### 10.5 Real-Time Updates

New messages are automatically fetched every 5 seconds while the chat is open. The chat auto-scrolls to the latest message when you are near the bottom of the conversation.

### 10.6 Navigation

- Click the **back arrow** to return to the contact list
- Click the **X button** to close the chat panel
- Click outside the panel to close it

## 11. HR Announcements & Notifications

### 11.1 Viewing Announcements

Click the **bell icon** in the header to open the announcements panel. A **red badge** shows the count of unread announcements.

Announcements appear with:
- Title and content
- Image (if attached)
- Author name and date
- Read/unread status

Announcements are automatically marked as read when you view them.

### 11.2 Creating Announcements (HR & Admin Only)

If you are in the HR department or have Admin role:

1. Click the bell icon to open the announcements panel
2. Click **"New Announcement"**
3. Enter:
   - **Title** — announcement heading
   - **Content** — full announcement text
   - **Image** — optional image attachment (max 5MB)
4. Click **"Publish"**

The announcement will be visible to all platform users.

### 11.3 Deleting Announcements

HR members and Admins can delete announcements by clicking the delete button on any announcement they created.

## 15. Admin Panel

The Admin Panel is accessible only to users with the **Admin** role. Click **"Admin Panel"** in the sidebar.

### 15.1 Overview Dashboard

Displays key platform metrics at a glance:
- **Total Users** — number of registered accounts
- **Saved Articles** — total bookmarked articles across all users
- **Research Items** — total workspace tracked items
- **New Users** — registrations in the last 7 days
- **Users by Department** — bar chart showing distribution

### 15.2 User Management

Manage all platform users:

- **Search users** by name, email
- **Filter** by department or role
- **View user table** showing name, email, department, role, and join date
- **Change user role** — click the role dropdown next to any user to set Viewer, Head, or Admin
- **Change department** — reassign a user to a different department
- **Delete user** — remove a user account (irreversible, also deletes their saved articles)

**Note:** You cannot modify or delete your own account from the Admin Panel.

### 15.3 Content Management

Monitor trending content:

- View the **most saved articles** across the platform
- See **save counts** per article (how many users bookmarked it)
- **Delete articles** from all users' saved collections if needed

### 15.4 Activity & KPI Tracking

Track team productivity and engagement:

**Filter Options:**
- **Time Range:** Today, This Week, This Month, or Custom date range
- **Department:** Filter by specific department or view all

**Metrics Tracked Per User:**

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Calls | Phone calls logged (via Call Log) |
| Items | Tracked items created in workspace |
| Done | Tracked items marked as completed |
| Notes | Notes written on tracked items |
| Saved | Articles bookmarked |
| Spravochnik | Knowledge base entries created |
| **Total** | Sum of all above activities |

**Summary Statistics:**
- Total actions across all users
- Most active user
- Average actions per user
- Number of active users

**Sorting:** Click any column header to sort the table by that metric.

**CSV Export:** Click **"Export CSV"** to download the activity data as a spreadsheet for reporting.
